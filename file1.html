Bakalakala Info...

The Bakalakala people are a group led by Daniel R, Chris Namba, and Raymond Lee. The group also has a council of ethics.

Weapons

The Hornet-The Hornet is a simple yet effective weapon that takes a V-shaped projectile and shoots it at high speeds in the direction pointed. You can put staples in it to make it more painful and sturdy, this is being mass developed. They are also working on the Hornet XXL.
